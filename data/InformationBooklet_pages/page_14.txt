4.3 Who is eligible for Merdeka Generation Subsidies?
Merdeka Generation seniors1 receive additional Merdeka Generation Subsidies
of 5% of their premiums, increasing to 10% from 76 years old and above,
regardless of their household income and Annual Value of their residences. This
is on top of the above premium subsidies that the seniors may receive.
